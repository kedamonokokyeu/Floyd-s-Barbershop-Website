About Us

Definition

Founded over 40 years ago, Floyd's has been serving its clients conveniently with its long amount of expertise. Located on Bascom Avenue of Campbell, Floyd's Barbershop's mission is to provide more than just a haircut; we aim to cultivate an experience that embodies tradition, craftsmanship, and community. With a commitment to excellence in grooming services, our skilled barbers are dedicated to delivering personalized styles that reflect the unique personality and preferences of each client.

Mission Statement

Beyond the chair, we foster a welcoming atmosphere in which friendships are formed and memories are made. Whether you're seeking a regular haircut or a modern makeover, Floyd's Barbershop is here to exceed your expectations and leave you looking and feeling your best. Visit us today and discover why Floyd's Barbershop is the premier destination for all your hair-related needs.
